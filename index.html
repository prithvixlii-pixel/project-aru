Farewell.

I do not even know if you care enough to revisit this page because I do not have the strength to do this in conversation. So this is where I will say what i want to communicate.
It is my own fault for being overly indulgent and intrusive in a place where it was not welcome.

I love you, but you already know that. You've known for about a year. I've been waiting for you to open your eyes and see that I was right here. And that I am more than a friend.
Thinking about you drinking coffee and a smile on your face because you are the most remarkable, maddening, challenging and frustrating person I have ever met. I love you.

I have been villianised, labelled irritating and an issue-bringer. Someone who's coming in between you and your happiness. But that is furthest from what is actually true and how I feel.
I never have to spend any kind of energy into thinking about you because you are always on my mind.
But I realise i will never even come close to you ever thinking about me. You do not even see me as a 'good enough' friend to you. We're cold and distant and the conversation, whatever there is to it feels like I am forcing it.

So, this is me, no more forcing what cannot be. This is me, wishing you everything your heart desires, all the love there is and some more, happiness and content.
I will not be an irritating presence in your life who pokes you about 'texts, calls or anything else.'
Getting to know you has been one of the most enriching experiences of my life, and I will always cherish it.

Not to make you any more uncomfortable, but I do love you.
Goodbye.
